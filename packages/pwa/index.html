Privacy & How It Works

How It Works

When Claude Code wants to do something (run a command, write a file), it sends a notification to this server, which immediately pushes it to your phone. You tap approve or deny, and Claude Code continues or stops.

Your Privacy

Your approval requests are automatically deleted after 60 seconds. They only exist long enough for you to make a decision. No data is stored permanently, no analytics are collected, and no tracking happens. This server just relays messages between Claude Code and your phone.
